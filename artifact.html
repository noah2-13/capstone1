The Best Foods in the World

In Defense of Global Gastronomy

The world is a smorgasbord of incredible dishes, each with its own story and flavor. From the sweet and savory to the spicy and sour, these iconic foods represent the culinary heart of their home countries and delight taste buds around the globe. The debate over which food reigns supreme is a fun, delicious exercise that can inspire travel, new experiences, and a deeper appreciation for diverse cultures.

Neapolitan Pizza (Italy)

The original pizza, with its simple yet high-quality ingredients—dough, tomatoes, salt, olive oil, and basil—cooked in a wood-fired oven, is a global phenomenon for a reason.

Sushi (Japan)

This Japanese delicacy of fresh fish and rice is a work of culinary art. Its clean flavors and artistic presentation have made it a healthy and beloved choice worldwide.

Massaman Curry (Thailand)

Hailed as a "king of curries," this dish combines spicy, sweet, savory, and coconut flavors to create a rich and complex taste sensation.

Peking Duck (China)

A maltose-syrup glaze gives this roasted duck its signature crispy skin. Served with pancakes and hoisin sauce, it's a dish celebrated for its succulent meat and incredible flavor.

Tacos (Mexico)

A fresh tortilla filled with grilled meat, guacamole, and salsa, the taco is a versatile and mouthwatering staple of Mexican cuisine.
